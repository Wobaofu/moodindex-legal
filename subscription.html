How to Subscribe

  1. Open the MoodGlobe app
  2. Tap on any Premium feature (e.g., "View 1 Year Trend")
  3. Select your preferred plan (Monthly or Lifetime)
  4. Confirm purchase with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
  5. Premium features unlock immediately

How to Cancel Your Monthly Subscription

To cancel on iPhone or iPad:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "MoodGlobe Premium"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
  6. Confirm cancellation

Important Notes:

  • Monthly subscription auto-renews unless cancelled
  • Cancelling stops future charges but you keep Premium features until the end of the current billing period
  • There are no partial refunds for unused time
  • You can resubscribe anytime
  • Lifetime Plan does not expire and does not auto-renew

Refund Policy

All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. We cannot process refunds directly.

To request a refund from Apple:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your MoodGlobe purchase
  4. Select "Request a Refund"
  5. Choose a reason and submit

Apple will review your request according to their refund policy. Approval is at Apple's discretion.
