Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service. We never sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Data: Country, city, age range, gender, occupation category, and income range (provided during onboarding)
  • Mood Data: Emotion type and intensity level for each check-in

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous User ID: Automatically generated by Firebase Authentication
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage patterns
  • Technical Data: Crash reports, performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide personalized mood insights and analytics
  • Generate aggregated global mood statistics (fully anonymized)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send optional notifications (only if you grant permission)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Google Cloud Firestore: For mood entries and profile data
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted both in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Region: Data is primarily stored in Asia-Pacific region

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

4.1 Aggregated Data

We share aggregated, anonymized mood statistics publicly within the app (e.g., "80% of users in Auckland reported positive moods today"). This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may have access to your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (Premium users only)
  • Apple: App Store analytics, crash reports

These services have their own privacy policies and data protection standards.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (contact us at zomeglobal@gmail.com)
  • Export: Download your mood data (Premium feature)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking in Settings
  • Correct: Update your profile information anytime

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your data as long as you use the app
  • If you uninstall and don't reinstall within 90 days, we may delete your data
  • You can request immediate deletion at any time
  • Aggregated statistics (anonymized) may be retained indefinitely

7. Children's Privacy

MoodIndex is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies in the mobile app. We use Firebase Analytics to track app usage, which you can opt out of in Settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if we have your contact)
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: zomeglobal@gmail.com
  • General Support: zomeglobal@gmail.com

Data Protection Officer: If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you may contact your local data protection authority.
